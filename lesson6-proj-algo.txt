The Rules:

1) Rock beats scissors.
2) Paper beats rock.
3) Scissors beat paper.


Rock-Paper-Scissors Algorithm

1) You make a move by clicking a button.
2) The computer makes a move at the same time as (1) above.
3) The two moves are compared to get a result.
4) The outcome, based on the rules of the game, is shown to the user via a pop-up.
